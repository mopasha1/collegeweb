SREENIDHI COLLEGE EVENTS

sreenidhi college has various events such as Rigolade,Adastra,Sreevisionetc

ADASTRA

ADASTRA is a Latin phrase that means, ‘REACHING FOR THE STARS’. It is a flagship event of IEEE – SNIST. Every new year begins with the coming of ADASTRA, which is not just a fest, but a 3-day celebration at SNIST.

SREEVISION

SREEVISION was initiated in 2009 by ISTE SNIST Chapter. It involves technical, cultural, sports, and many other non-technical events. MIRTH is the cultural night which is an intercollege cultural pavement that provides students with enormous enthusiasm as a part of their overall personality development.
